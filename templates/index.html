About the Job

  • 1 Provide technical support (all-levels) to customers via phone, email and through our ticketing system;
  • 2 Respond to service requests in a timely manner (meeting or exceeding our contracted SLAs)
  • 3 Troubleshoot and repair client computers systems (mostly PC, some MAC, smartphones)
  • 4 Act as the point of contact for our clients on all types of requests generated internally via our monitoring systems or externally direct from clients.
  • 5 Properly classify (priority/categorize) all service requests within our ticket system.
  • 6 Coordinate additional resources needed to accomplish requested tasks including client vendors.
  • 7 Communicate with customers and additional resources to notify them as to the status of their requests or needed repairs.
  • 8 Escalate service requests which you cannot resolve to meet client expectations.
  • 9 Management of Client Backup & Disaster Recovery Solutions (SolarWinds, Datto, MSP360, StorageCraft)
  • 10 Manage and Administer IIS on VPS or Azure cloud

Additional Responsibilities:

  • 1 Complete training programs for our ticketing and monitoring systems.
  • 2 Recommend improvements to our internal processes to maximize customer service levels
  • 3 Complete time sheets daily with detailed notes of work completed
  • 4 Develop rapport with clients as a trusted resource for help
  • 5 Educate end-users on hardware and software operations as needed
  • 6 Should have good understanding about configuration of Network switches / firewalls

Qualifications and Requirements:

  • 1 Minimum of 5 - 7 years' experience in similar technology support role required.
  • 2 MCP, MCTS, or higher certifications required
  • 3 Minimum 1 year of past employment experience in a customer service role
